"Te Whare Tapa Whā" wellbeing plan

18 September 2023

What is Te Whare Tapa whā?

Te Whare Tapa Whā is a Māori model of wellbeing, likened to a four-sided house, presenting four interconnected dimensions: physical health (Taha Tinana), spiritual wellbeing (Taha Wairua), family connections (Taha Whānau), and mental and emotional wellness (Taha Hinengaro), plus the Whenua (land) which forms the foundation for the four dimensions.

The model emphasizes the necessity of balance and integration of these dimensions for overall health. It's a culturally grounded approach to understanding health within the Māori context, offering a holistic perspective essential for addressing health issues in the Māori community and beyond.

My Wellbeing Plan

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Focus on physical health, exercise, nutrition, rest, and relaxation.

  • Go for a run 3 times per week
  • Eat balanced meals (whole plant foods, lean proteins and healthy fats)
  • Keep to a consistent sleep schedule

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Addresses mental and emotional health, stress management, personal growth, and psychological wellbeing.

  • Listen to a guided meditation every day
  • Take regular breaks while studying
  • Spend time with friends

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Emphasizes the spiritual self, beliefs, connection to nature, and the universe.

  • Spend time outdoors in parks
  • Read, listen to podcasts/audiobooks on ethics, philosophy, science, social issues, and psychology.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Highlights the importance of healthy relationships with family, friends, and community involvement.

  • Keep in touch with whānau
  • Meet up with friends at least once a week
  • Attend networking meetups/conferences

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Increase the connection to the land and environment, promoting sustainability, outdoor engagement, and understanding the cultural importance of the land.

  • Engage in outdoor activities like hiking and camping
  • Reduce waste, recycle, and minimize your ecological footprint
