Bootcamp Learning Plan

  1. What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

    My goal is to work in a tech company where I can contribute to creating innovative applications and platforms, drawing on my previous experience in business analysis, supply chain management and IT training.. I envision myself developing user-friendly products that simplify complex tasks and enhance user experiences.

  2. What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

    A couple of my strengths are my attention to detail and the pride I take in my work. This ensures a high standard of output; however, it can also be a limitation as it may lead me to over-complicate tasks and spend excessive time perfecting them. To mitigate this, I intend to set clear time benchmarks for each task, reminding myself that achieving a good result within a reasonable timeframe is crucial. It's about finding a balance between maintaining quality and avoiding unnecessary perfectionism.

  3. What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

    One of the main challenges I anticipate during Bootcamp is maintaining a healthy work-life balance while fully immersing myself in the program. Having the right balance between dedicating ample time to learning and ensuring personal well-being is a crucial aspect for me. It's important to prioritize self-care, manage time effectively, and stay organized to tackle the challenge successfully.

  4. What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    I aspire to enhance my communication and teamwork skills during my time at Dev Academy. Effective communication and the ability to work collaboratively within a team are vital in the tech industry. I also aim to improve my self-awareness, enabling me to adapt and grow both personally and professionally.

  5. What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

    I expect the Bootcamp team to provide a supportive and motivating learning environment. Clear communication, guidance, and constructive feedback will be crucial for my growth during Bootcamp. I hope for a collaborative atmosphere where everyone can learn from each other.

  6. What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

    During Bootcamp, I will proactively seek help when needed, utilizing available resources such as mentors, fellow students, and online documentation. I commit to managing my workload by setting achievable daily goals, regularly reviewing my progress, and adjusting my plan as necessary. Additionally, I'll actively engage in group activities, contribute my ideas, and ensure a respectful and inclusive interaction with peers and facilitators.
